The What

DowncityJS is a 24 hour JavaScript Hackathon which will take place in Providence, RI. We'll provide the space, connectivity, food, and drinks. You provide the code.

The Who

We're looking for motivated JavaScript developers and web designers to come together and build the best web application or library they can in a 24-hour period.

The When

DowncityJS will take place on February 2nd, 2013.
